How to use this tool?

It is very simple, all you have to do is choose a photoluminescene decay curve (lifetime) file from your computer by clicking the top left button "Choose file", open the chosen file, the average lifetime result will appear right in the top-right red box, the decay curve will be displyed in the bottom-left box, and all the numerical values involved in the calculations will appear in the bottom-right text area. The average lifetime unit (s, ms, us, ns) will always be the unit of your measurements.
